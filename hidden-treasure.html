Hidden Treasures

(CHILD RIGHTS TO EDUCATION CAMPAIGN)

Nations are build on the foundation laid by the elders but strengthened by the generations to come, with this belief, we value education and the power of knowledge is the greatest gift of them that there can be. With Educations comes Independence, value of freedom, discipline and ambition to do great and also contribute towards the nation’s growth as a payback in the long run and time to come. Today’s youth and children will become leaders of tomorrow representing our great nation to the world. And it’s our moral duty to empower them with the right basic and knowledge which will help them to continue the legacy of this great nation for many more generations to come. India as a nation over the last two decade and more has been majorly focusing on the education and literacy aspect among its youth and adults, Knowledge and Literacy are the key component of a strong and a stable nation.

"Two gifts are especially appreciated, the gift of learning and the gift of life. But the gift of learning takes precedence. One may save a man's life, and that is excellent; one may impart to knowledge, and that is better.” - Swami Vivekananda

We, at GIFTS FOUNDATION INDIA have pledge to contribute towards our nations development and to identify and help as many children as possible to win the fight in their respective battle of survival and life. Representing expert tie – ups with the belief of changing life with the power of knowledge and making as many youth and children come forward help to us change the faith of this great nation. We, at GIFTS FOUNDATION INDIA are sure to change lives with our initiative “HIDDEN TREASURS”

Did You Know?

India with the current literacy rate of 74% is still far behind of many other countries smaller and less populated yet successful with better standards of living, as per the UNICEF data India ranges between the positions of 100 – 108 over the last decade in its Adult Literacy rate which leave a lot of room for us to grow in this domain.

“These little children are unaware about their hidden talents and surely unaware about the medium of respective contribution to this country, Lets enable all with an equal opportunity to find their true potential. Help them find themselves with the power of knowledge & Education.”
