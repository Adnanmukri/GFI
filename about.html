GIFTS FOUNDATION INDIA

(Gender Initiatives For True Sustainability Foundations)

Gifts India is a humanitarian organization dedicated to work on the issues of Gender Inequality & Discrimination, with dedicated flagship programs and with an ambition along with a motive to build a nation with the best possible standards of living and prosperity, we strive very hard to eradicate poverty and discrimination within the nation by building a bridge for humanity to come through and have an equal opportunity.


Gifts India recognizes the disproportionate distribution of opportunities and power, constantly resulting into violation of civil and social rights. We also understand the possibilities which arise from such a imbalance.

Our Mission

To be inclusive and exclusive in the formation of a better nation with our programmatic theme and goals for a more equal opportunity awarding nation, upbringing and uplifting educational qualities and eradicating discrimination and poverty across the country.

Our Vision

To have more stronger and equipped nation empowering its people with equal opportunities across all domains. In India we seek a nation which awards its people with opportunities to realize their dreams without any discrimination whatsoever.

Our Objective

To be one the leading development organization of the nation, through our flagship campaign customized to meet demands and we intend to make an impact on the most marginalized communities of our country to eradicate poverty and injustice whilst empowering them with knowledge, skills and proper resources, making them self realized and self dependant
