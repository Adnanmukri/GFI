Safina

(Women Empowerment)

Women Empowerment is a global issue and is the need for providing equal opportunity has been accepted and accredited by many nations across the globe, women constitute to almost half of the population in the world, which makes them responsible and equal contenders with the other half. Women across the global have stepped forward to participate in the global reforms in all sector and continuing to increase their share of the contribution towards greater objectives and success.

Women Empowerment has also become one of the most efficient medium of reducing poverty across the globe , the additional component money or livelihood from the female member of the family when added to the male component has meeting family demands more easier . It has also proven to have reduced the rates of domestic violence; educated women stand a far less chance to encounter an instance of domestic violence as compared to uneducated one. Women are equally competent. Nowadays, women are even ahead of men in many socio-economic activities.

“Women are the largest reservoir of talent in the world” – Hilary Clinton – Presidential Candidate - United States -2016.

We, at GIFTS FOUNDATION INDIA have pledge to empower women and girls to identify their rights and acknowledge their duties towards self , family , society , community and at large the nation. We, understand the untapped pool of talent waiting to be discovered in these women who have the potential but lack an opportunity, we would like them to have this opportunity through our flagship campaign – SAFINA, with expert partners and specialist working on the issue on women empowerment. We encourage women and girls across the nation towards educations, livelihood opportunities and towards being a more independent and equal contributor in the success.

  • • Microfinance for Small Medium Women Entrepreneurs.

Did You Know?

According to the Global Gender Gap Index 2016, India ranks 87 with a rating of 0.683 on the matrix of equality where the highest possible score on the matrix is 1 (equality) and the lowest possible score is 0 (inequality).
