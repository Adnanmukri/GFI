FAQ

Why Women & Girls?

At, Gifts we understand that women constitutes to almost half of world’s populations and with these lack in opportunities we have a great reservoir of untapped pool of talent which could be very resourceful in the times to come for the development of this great nation. Hence , we choose to focus on women and girls equipping them with the power of knowledge and resource, because based on a our experience it has proven that when women are powerful, they prove to help not only themselves but the whole family and entire community . We know women can play a vital role in eradicating poverty from this nation.

Why Children?

At, GIFTS we also understand as one of the youngest nations in the world and a larger proportion of our population being children. We award these children with the opportunity of early development opportunities, with access to knowledge through education for a bright future.

We believe in equal rights to all and work with the most marginalized people of the nation through well planned initiatives and comprehensive programs in partnership with the industry experts, spread across the country. We work for all with no discrimination what so ever in regards with religion, caste, creed or gender in both sector of development, Urban as well as Rural. We achieve our objectives through medium of Education, Women and Girl Child Empowerment along with Skill Development for better livelihood and Medical Aid for the needy.
